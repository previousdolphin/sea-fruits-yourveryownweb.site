The Philosophy

Nature Had More Imagination Than We Gave It Credit For

At Sea Fruits, we work with a small network of tidal orchards — coastal plots where the boundary between the sea floor and fertile earth has blurred over millennia. The creatures that emerge from these liminal waters are unlike anything found in a conventional fish market.

Our chefs treat each hybrid with the reverence of a wild ingredient: minimal intervention, maximal respect for what the sea — and the tree — intended.
